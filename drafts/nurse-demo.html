Talk to a registered nurse

Registered nurses are accessible by phone—whether you have a VYEPTI prescription or are considering it as a treatment option. Get answers to your questions and help ease concerns by talking to a real nurse.

For any questions that are specific to you and your situation, ask your healthcare provider.

Nurse availability

Live registered nurses are available Monday through Friday, 8 AM-8 PM (ET).

[callout]

Call 833-4-VYEPTI (833-489-3784) Option 2 to connect live with a registered nurse.

Not yet prescribed VYEPTI? No problem. A nurse is happy to answer your questions.

IMPORTANT SAFETY INFORMATION

[callout]

Do not receive VYEPTI if you have a known allergy to eptinezumab-jjmr or its ingredients.

APPROVED USE

[callout]

VYEPTI is a prescription medicine used for the preventive treatment of migraine in adults.
