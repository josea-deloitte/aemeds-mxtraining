Dosing for VYEPTI and other preventive migraine treatment options

VYEPTI is a preventive treatment for migraine that’s delivered differently. Check out how often these preventive migraine treatments are given.

This is not a comparison of how well each treatment works or how safe they may be.

IV infusion for migraine

VYEPTI® (eptinezumab-jjmr)

4x a year

One 30-minute infusion administered by a healthcare provider 4x a year

Migraine injections

Aimovig® (erenumab-aooe)

12x a year

One self-injection 1x a month

AJOVY® (fremanezumab-vfrm)

12x or 4x a year

One self-injection 1x a month or 3 self-injections, 4x a year

BOTOX® (onabotulinumtoxinA)

4x a year

31 injections at each treatment visit, 4x a year

Emgality® (galcanezumab-gnlm)

12x a year

One self-injection 1x a month (after initial loading dose*)

Migraine pills

Nurtec® ODT (rimegepant)

182x a year

One pill every other day

QULIPTA® (atogepant)

365x a year

One pill 1x a day

*The first dose is a loading dose and may be given as 1 or 2 injections.

The above is not a complete list of preventive treatments of migraine in adults. The comparison pertains only to differences in dosing and administration and should not be considered a comparison of efficacy or safety.
