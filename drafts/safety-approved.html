IMPORTANT SAFETY INFORMATION

Do not receive VYEPTI if you have a known allergy to eptinezumab-jjmr or its ingredients.

Common side effects may include:

  • Nasopharyngitis (common cold)
  • Sinusitis
  • Upper respiratory tract infection

[callout]

Serious Side Effects: If you experience difficulty breathing, severe allergic reactions, or chest pain, seek immediate medical attention.

Tell your healthcare provider if you are pregnant, planning to become pregnant, or breastfeeding.

APPROVED USE

VYEPTI is a prescription medicine used for the preventive treatment of migraine in adults.

How it works: VYEPTI is a monoclonal antibody that binds to calcitonin gene-related peptide (CGRP) in the body.

[callout]

Dosing: VYEPTI is administered as an intravenous (IV) infusion. Your healthcare provider will determine the appropriate dose for your condition.

Frequency: Typically administered every 12 weeks.

Contraindications

VYEPTI should not be used in patients with:

  • Known hypersensitivity to the drug or components
  • Active infections requiring treatment

[callout]

Critical Warning: Do not use without consulting your physician if you have immunosuppression or recent vaccinations.

Drug Interactions

VYEPTI may interact with other medications. Always inform your healthcare provider about all medications you are taking, including:

  • Other migraine treatments
  • Immunosuppressants
  • Live vaccines
